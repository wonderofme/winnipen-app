# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [Date]**

## 1. Introduction

Winnipen ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Account information (username, email, profile picture)
- Posts, comments, and other content you create
- Location data when you post or interact with the app
- Communications with us

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- Device information (device type, operating system, app version)
- Usage data (features used, time spent in app)
- Location data (with your permission)
- Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- Google account information (when you sign in with Google)
- Firebase authentication data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Authenticate your identity
- Show you relevant posts based on your location
- Enable real-time communication features
- Improve our services
- Respond to your inquiries
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 With Other Users
- Your username and profile picture (unless in anonymous mode)
- Posts and comments you create
- General location data (approximate area, not exact coordinates)

### 4.2 With Service Providers
- Firebase (for authentication and storage)
- MongoDB Atlas (for data storage)
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights and property
- To prevent fraud or abuse
- To protect user safety

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication systems
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access and Update
- You can view and update your profile information
- You can change your privacy settings
- You can enable anonymous mode for posts

### 7.2 Data Portability
- You can request a copy of your data
- You can export your posts and comments

### 7.3 Deletion
- You can delete individual posts and comments
- You can request complete account deletion
- We will delete your data within 30 days of request

## 8. Location Data

- Location data is essential for the App's functionality
- We only collect location data when you post or interact with location-based features
- Location data is not shared with third parties except as described in this policy
- You can disable location services, but this will limit app functionality

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

## 10. International Data Transfers
